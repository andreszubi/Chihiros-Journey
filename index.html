Chihiro's Journey

Instructions

Click anywhere to start music and begin your journey!

Chihiro has gotten lost in the spirit world and she must make her way back home!
She must eat the eggs to remain invisible while crossing the bridge.
She must get across the bridge safely by avoiding the spirits and eating the food along the way.
There is a timer that will show how long it will take her to get to the end.
However, there is ramen noodle soups which will will drop and they will take
Time away from the timer, making you win the game faster.

You may use your arrow keys on your keyboard in order to make Chihiro move up,down, left and right.

Oh no! You have been caught by the spirits
and they are ready to feast on you!

Congratulations! You have escaped from the spirits!
